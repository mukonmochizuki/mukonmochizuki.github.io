Lessons, Workshops, & Collaborations

Mukon Mochizuki’s mission is to expand awareness of the traditional Japanese performing arts through a variety of experiences. Whether you are seeking long-term tutelage or a short intensive during your travels, Mukon offers specialized instruction tailored to your background.

Inquire About Lessons

Private Lessons

Mukon offers opportunities for private lessons, tailored to the unique needs and experience levels of his students. These sessions are highly ideal for:

  • Expats living in Japan long-term who desire in-depth and hands-on experiences in the hōgaku bayashi.
  • Exchange students or those studying abroad looking for authentic cultural music experiences.

Group Intensives

Visiting taiko groups and taiko ensembles abroad looking for intensive sessions and residencies tailored to their specific needs are especially encouraged to reach out!

Themes can include first-time workshops in hōgaku bayashi using actual traditional instruments, or exploring how to learn hōgaku bayashi and fuse it effectively with naname or kumi daiko performance.
